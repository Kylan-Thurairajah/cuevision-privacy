CueVision Privacy Policy

Effective date: September 2025

Information We Collect

CueVision collects photos and text messages that are voluntarily sent to the CueVision Assistant Facebook Page. These messages may include images taken with Meta Ray-Bans or other devices.

How We Use Your Information

We use this information solely for the purpose of analyzing images with computer vision tools in order to provide shot recommendations for billiards and pool. Data is processed in real time and may be temporarily stored for debugging or research purposes.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Access is restricted to the CueVision development team for the sole purpose of improving the service.

Data Retention & Deletion

Images and messages are stored only as long as necessary to provide the requested analysis. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at: cuevision.team@example.com.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at cuevision.team@example.com.
